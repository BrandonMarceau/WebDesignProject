Meet Our Team

Jane Smith - CEO

Jane Smith graduated from Colorado University with a master's degree in business management in 2007. After a few different jobs working in the corporate business world, she decided to start her own company and opened Colorado Denim in 2012. Ever since, she has dedicated herself to ensure the highest quality products are being sold with the Colorado Denim name.

Ted Smith - Manufacturing Lead

Ted Smith has a quality manufacturing degree with a minor in enviromental science, making him the perfect fit as a manufacturing lead that can keep a clean manufacturing process in mind.

Jim Smith - Sales Lead

Jim Smith has a strong background in sales working for several other large clothing companies before joining the family business. Jim ensures that products are fairly priced and affordable for our customers.

Sue Smith - Customer Satisfaction

Sue Smith is all about you. Her main focus is ensuring that every customer that buys and wears our products is 100% satisfied. If any customers are not satisfied Sue is always available to meet 1 on 1 and discuss any problems they have.
